# GapStay GTM Playbook

## Positioning

GapStay is revenue recovery for Airbnb and VRBO hosts with empty 14-90 day calendar gaps.

Do not position it as a marketplace. Do not position it as cheaper Airbnb. Position it as a host-side recovery workflow:

> You have empty Airbnb dates. GapStay turns them into a realistic furnished-stay offer you can share anywhere.

## First paid user

The first paid user is a frustrated short-term rental host with one dead calendar gap.

They are not buying software. They are trying to recover revenue from a specific empty block of dates.

## Wedge offer

Free:
- Analyze one gap
- See fantasy revenue vs likely Airbnb outcome
- Preview the recommended furnished-stay offer

Paid entry:
- $19 to publish one active GapStay page for 30 days

Expansion:
- $49/month for one property
- $99/month for up to 10 properties
- Later: 3% to 7% success fee if GapStay handles booking/payment

## Core message

Primary headline:

> Fill the empty month.

Subhead:

> GapStay turns vacant Airbnb and VRBO dates into realistic furnished-stay offers hosts can publish and share anywhere.

Pain-led ad copy:

> Your $8,000 Airbnb gap may not be worth $8,000. See what it is actually worth as a mid-term stay.

Direct CTA:

> Analyze my gap free.

## ICP

Start with hosts in oversupplied or seasonal STR markets:
- Florida vacation markets
- Arizona
- Tennessee
- Carolinas
- Texas lake/beach markets
- Any market where hosts complain about the Airbnb bust

Best early users:
- 1-3 property hosts
- small property managers
- hosts with upcoming 14-90 day vacancies
- hosts already considering Furnished Finder or Facebook groups
- hosts who are angry at Airbnb but not ready to quit hosting

## Channels

### 1. Facebook groups

Target groups:
- Airbnb host groups
- short-term rental host groups
- local landlord groups
- travel nurse housing groups
- relocation housing groups
- insurance displacement housing groups

Post angle:

> I built a free calculator that shows whether an empty Airbnb month is better sold as a furnished mid-term stay. Looking for hosts with a 14-90 day gap to test it.

### 2. Reddit and forums

Targets:
- r/AirBnBHosts
- r/airbnb_hosts
- r/realestateinvesting
- BiggerPockets forums
- local investor forums

Do not spam product links. Use diagnostic posts:

> How are hosts pricing empty 30-60 day gaps right now? Nightly discounts seem to miss the point.

Then offer the calculator to commenters.

### 3. Direct host outreach

Find Airbnb listings with visible future gaps manually at first. Contact hosts through public channels where available.

Outbound message:

> I noticed your calendar has a long open block. GapStay estimates what that gap could earn as a 30-60 day furnished stay and creates a page you can share outside Airbnb. Free to analyze; $19 only if you publish the page.

### 4. Property managers

Target managers with 10-100 STR units in soft markets.

Pitch:

> We find awkward vacancy gaps across your portfolio and turn them into mid-term stay offers before they become dead inventory.

### 5. SEO

Initial content topics:
- how to fill empty Airbnb calendar
- Airbnb occupancy down what to do
- Airbnb mid term rental pricing
- Airbnb long gaps between bookings
- Airbnb bust host strategy
- Furnished Finder vs Airbnb for hosts
- how to price a 30 day furnished rental

Each article should drive to the free gap analyzer.

## Activation metric

The key activation event is not signup.

Activation is:

> Host sees the side-by-side math for one real gap and clicks publish/preview.

## Conversion metric

Initial paid conversion:

> Host pays $19 to publish one gap page.

## Retention metric

Retention comes from repeat gaps:

> Host returns when another 14-90 day vacancy appears.

## VC framing

GapStay is not competing with Airbnb demand. It monetizes distressed supply that Airbnb is failing to clear.

Strategic acquirers could care because the product reduces host churn, expands mid-term supply, and creates a bridge between STR inventory and furnished housing demand.
