## Objection handling

Objection: I do not want another subscription.

Answer: That is why the first paid step is not a subscription. Analyze free. Pay $19 only if you want to publish one specific gap page for 30 days.

Objection: I already use Airbnb.

Answer: GapStay does not replace Airbnb. It recovers dates Airbnb is not clearing efficiently.

Objection: I can just use Furnished Finder.

Answer: Furnished Finder is a demand channel. GapStay helps price and package the specific calendar gap before you share it anywhere.

Objection: I do not want long-term tenants.

Answer: GapStay focuses on 14-90 day furnished stays, not annual leases. The host controls the offer, dates, and screening.

## Success metric

The early signal is not total signups. It is hosts who enter a real gap and say: I would publish this.
